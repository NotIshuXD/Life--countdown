Ever feel guilty about using plastic?

Many companies make you feel responsible for saving the planet, but the truth is—they might still be polluting behind the scenes. Let’s uncover who’s really responsible and find better choices together!

Spot the Greenwashing!

Ad 1: “Eco-Friendly Bottle”

Company claims 100% recycled material, but they produce tons of plastic elsewhere.

Truth: They are still polluting heavily! Not fully eco-friendly.

Ad 2: “Save the Planet Campaign”

Encourages customers to recycle, but the company doesn’t reduce its plastic production.

Truth: Company needs real accountability!

Ad 3: “Green Packaging”

Packaging says biodegradable, but most of it ends up in landfills or incinerators.

Truth: Labels can be misleading! Check carefully.

Support Real Eco-Friendly Products

Bamboo Water Bottle – Fully natural and reusable.

Cloth Bags – Replace plastic bags for shopping.

Organic Soap – Biodegradable and locally made.

Tips & Actions

  • Check labels: Not everything “eco-friendly” really is.
  • Support local brands over flashy ads.
  • Recycle properly but don’t take all the blame.
  • Share what you learn with friends and family.
  • Be a smart consumer: Spot misleading campaigns!
